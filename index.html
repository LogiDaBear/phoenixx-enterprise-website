WHO WE ARE EMPOWERING COMMUNITIES

Phoenixx Enterprises is a full-service safety and security training consultancy based in Columbus, OH. We are trans owned and operated and dedicated to bridging the gaps in our communities through education, communication, and visibility.

Our mission is simple yet powerful: to empower individuals and organizations with the knowledge and skills needed to save lives. Through comprehensive training programs and security services, we ensure that when moments matter most, you're prepared to rise.
